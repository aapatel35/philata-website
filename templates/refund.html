Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2025
💰

Fair Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with our services. If you're not happy, you may be eligible for a refund depending on the work completed. Refunds are issued for unused services only. Government fees are non-refundable.

1. Overview

This Refund and Cancellation Policy outlines the terms under which Philata Immigration Services Inc. will provide refunds for services purchased.

This Policy applies to all services offered by Philata, including immigration consultations, application preparation, document review, and profile assessments.

Important:

This Policy should be read in conjunction with your Retainer Agreement, which may contain additional specific terms regarding refunds for your particular service.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Services Eligible for Refund

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund if:

  • You cancel services before we begin work on your case
  • We are unable to provide the services as described in your Retainer Agreement
  • Significant delays occur due to our error or negligence

2.2 Services NOT Eligible for Refund

No refund will be provided for:

  • Work already completed
  • Government processing fees paid on your behalf
  • Third-party fees (language tests, medical exams, police certificates, courier fees)
  • Application refusals or rejections by IRCC or provincial authorities
  • Changes in immigration policy beyond our control
  • Your decision to withdraw after we've started work
  • Failure to provide required documents or information on time
No Guarantee of Approval:

We cannot guarantee that your immigration application will be approved. Refunds are NOT provided if your application is refused by immigration authorities. The final decision on all applications rests with IRCC or the relevant provincial body.

3. Refund Schedule

Refund amounts are calculated based on the stage of work completed:

Stage of Service Work Completed Refund Amount
Before Work Begins Retainer paid, no work started 100% minus $299 admin fee
Initial Assessment Profile reviewed, eligibility assessed 80% of remaining balance
Document Preparation Forms filled, documents collected 50% of remaining balance
Application Submitted Application filed with IRCC/province No refund
Post-Submission Support Application submitted, ongoing monitoring No refund

4. Refund Request Process

4.1 How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Submit a written request via email to refunds@philata.ca
  2. Include: Your full name, Client ID or case number, Service purchased, Reason for refund request
  3. Allow 14 business days for us to review your request

4.2 Refund Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method:

  • Credit Card: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days
  • PayPal: 3-5 business days

5. Government and Third-Party Fees

Non-Refundable Fees:

The following fees are NEVER refundable under any circumstances:

  • IRCC processing fees
  • Provincial nominee program fees
  • Biometric fees
  • Language test fees (IELTS, CELPIP, TEF)
  • Educational Credential Assessment (ECA) fees
  • Medical examination fees
  • Police certificate fees
  • Translation service fees

These fees are paid directly to government agencies or third-party service providers and cannot be refunded by Philata.
