1. Introduction

Philata Immigration Services Inc. ("Philata," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website philata.ca or use our services.

Compliance:

This Privacy Policy complies with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), the European Union's General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and other applicable privacy laws.

We collect information that identifies, relates to, describes, or could reasonably be linked with you:

👤

Identity Information

  • Full name
  • Date of birth
  • Citizenship/nationality
  • Passport number
📧

Contact Information

  • Email address
  • Phone number(s)
  • Mailing address
  • Country of residence
🎓

Immigration-Related

  • Education history
  • Work experience
  • Language test scores
  • Family information
💻

  • IP address

When you use our free online tools, we may collect the following information:

📈

  • Age and marital status

  • Language test scores
  • Work experience details
  • Calculated CRS score
🕑

  • Categories browsed
  • Session duration
📝

  • Test responses
  • Score results

2.3 Sensitive Personal Information

We may collect sensitive information necessary for immigration services:
  • Criminal history or police certificates
  • Medical information (only if required for applications)
  • Marital status and family relationships
  • Financial status (proof of funds)

We only collect sensitive information with your explicit consent and when necessary for your immigration application.

7. Your Privacy Rights

Under PIPEDA, GDPR, and other privacy laws, you have the following rights:

Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances. Note: We must retain data required for legal/regulatory compliance.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a machine-readable format (CSV or JSON).

Right to Withdraw Consent

If we process data based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time.

Response Time:

We will respond to privacy rights requests within 30 days. To exercise your rights, email privacy@philata.ca.
