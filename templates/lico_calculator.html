About LICO Requirements

To sponsor parents/grandparents, you must meet the LICO+30% (Low Income Cut-Off plus 30%) income threshold for 3 consecutive tax years. Your income must be equal to or greater than the LICO amount for your total family size, which includes you, your spouse/partner, dependents, AND the people you're sponsoring.
