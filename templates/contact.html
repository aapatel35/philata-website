Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Philata.

What is Philata?

Philata is an AI-powered platform that automates Canadian immigration content creation. It monitors IRCC updates in real-time, generates platform-specific captions, and creates professional visuals for social media.

How accurate is the content?

All content is verified against official IRCC sources. Our AI systems cross-reference information to ensure accuracy, and we always include source citations for transparency.

Which platforms are supported?

Philata generates content optimized for Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X. Each platform gets tailored captions that follow best practices for that specific platform.

How fast are updates detected?

Our systems monitor IRCC and other official sources 24/7. Express Entry draws and major announcements are typically detected and content is generated within minutes of official release.

Can I customize the content?

Yes! All generated content is available in our Content Hub where you can preview, review, and make any adjustments before posting to your social media channels.
